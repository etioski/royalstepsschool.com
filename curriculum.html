Royal Steps Curriculum

Royal Steps International School uses a hybrid Curriculum which comprises:
International Primary Curriculum(IPC)
and
Nigerian Curriculum
Nigerian Curriculum
The Nigerian Curriculum provides Royal Steps students with knowledge and skills about our host country. It also prepares students to be adequately equipped for their secondary education. Students that have been introduced to the curriculum in their primary education are better placed and prepared for the West African Examination Council when they get to secondary level. ​

International Primary Curriculum(IPC)
This curriculum has been developed to provide support to teachers and enhance their teaching skills so that the following goals can be achieved: ​
To help children learn the subject knowledge, skills and understandings they need to become aware of the world around them.
To help children develop the personal skills they need to take an active part in the world throughout their lives
To help children develop an international mindset alongside awareness of their own nationality
To do each of these in ways which take into account up to date research into how children learn and how they can be encouraged to be lifelong learners
The themes of the IPC can be applied to all subjects such as Science, Art, Technology, Information and Communication Technology, Mathematics and Social Studies.
